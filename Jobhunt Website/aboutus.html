About Us

So, what is Grabmyjob?



We are a thriving community for workplace conversations,
driven by a simple mission: helping people everywhere find
jobs and companies they love. But the way we do it ?
That's not so simple.

Every day, We are inspired by a vision to make positive workplace
change through radical transparency. Through the products we make and
the communities we create, we are breaking down barriers that lead to
discrimination, pay gaps, and toxic work environments. Together, we are
fostering a world where people have the support and resources they need to find a job that loves them back.
